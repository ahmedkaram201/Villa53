a world about us

Villa 53 was established in 1985 and since then we have been serving the best meals in the oriental kitchen prepared in a special way that guarantees that you will like it and you will want to visit us again. We focus on preparing distinctive dishes in shawarma, grills and fattah. Our dishes contain special ingredients prepared by expert chefs so that our customers can enjoy a delicious meal.

our delicious menu

01.Fried chicken fatteh

the components Muhammara chicken, toasted bread, yellow dora setting method The chicken is well seasoned in our special spices and sauce, then cooked until it turns golden and distinctive in the dish with white basmati rice.

02.Half grilled chicken with white rice and red sauce

The chicken is placed in the marinade for a period of no less than 12 hours until it has a distinctive taste, then it is placed on charcoal until it is cooked well and served in a dish with basmati rice with vegetables and sauce.

03.Seafood Fattah

We cook some of the best choices of seafood from calamar, and shrimps with our specially made sauces. Served on a platter white rice with toasted bread and garlic sauce, then the seafood on top to drop the juice it has on the rice and delivers a great experience
